Privacy Policy

We value your privacy. This policy details how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Privacy Policy Overview

This Privacy Policy applies to the Klotski puzzle game website (https://klotski.org) and all related services. We are committed to protecting your privacy rights and ensuring you understand how we handle your personal information.

Important Notice

By using our services, you agree to the data processing methods described in this Privacy Policy. If you disagree with any terms of this policy, please stop using our services.

Information We Collect

1. Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect the following information:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, browser type and version
  • Usage Data: Access times, page view history, game progress data
  • Technical Data: IP address, cookie information, browser settings
  • Performance Data: Page load times, error logs, performance metrics

2. Information You Provide

When you use our contact form or participate in interactive features, you may provide:

  • Name and email address (for contact and feedback)
  • Game preference settings and custom configurations
  • Feedback and comments
  • Technical support and issue reports

3. Third-Party Service Information

We use the following third-party services that may collect related information:

  • Google Analytics: Website visit statistics and analysis
  • Pageview.app: Website traffic monitoring
  • Browser Local Storage: Game progress saving

How We Use Information

1. Providing Services

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and improve Klotski game services
  • Save and restore your game progress
  • Personalize your gaming experience
  • Process your feedback and requests

2. Website Optimization

We use analytics data to:

  • Improve website performance and user experience
  • Identify and fix technical issues
  • Optimize game features and interface design
  • Understand user usage patterns and preferences

3. Communication

If you provide contact information, we may:

  • Respond to your inquiries and feedback
  • Send important service update notifications
  • Provide technical support and assistance

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We Do Not Sell Your Personal Information

We commit to not selling, renting, or trading your personal information to third parties.

Limited Information Sharing

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party service providers who help us operate the website
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Security Protection: To prevent fraud, abuse, or security threats
  • Business Transfers: During company mergers, acquisitions, or asset transfers

Third-Party Services

The third-party services we use (such as Google Analytics) have their own privacy policies. We recommend you review these services' privacy policies to understand how they handle your data.

Data Security

Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information:

  • Use HTTPS encryption for data transmission
  • Implement access controls and authentication
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Employee privacy training

Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary:

  • Game progress data: Stored locally on your device, controlled by you
  • Analytics data: Typically retained for no more than 2 years
  • Contact information: Retained until you request deletion

Your Rights

Access and Update

You have the right to:

  • Access personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Restrict or object to data processing

Cookie Control

You can control cookie usage through browser settings:

  • Disable or delete cookies
  • Set cookie preferences
  • Opt out of analytics tracking

Contact Us

If you want to exercise these rights or have any privacy-related questions, please contact us through the following methods:

Privacy-Related Contact

Email: contact@klotski.org

Website: https://klotski.org

We will respond to your request within 30 days of receipt.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not specifically targeted at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

International Data Transfers

Our services may involve transferring your information to servers outside your country/region. We ensure these transfers comply with applicable data protection laws and implement appropriate protective measures.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be notified to you through:

  • Posting update notices on the website
  • Sending email notifications (if you provided an email address)
  • Updating the policy effective date

We recommend you regularly review this policy to understand any changes.
