Frequently Asked Questions

What should I do if the game won't load?
Please check your internet connection and ensure your browser supports JavaScript. If the problem persists, try clearing your browser cache or using a different browser.
How do I save my game progress?
Click the "Save Game" button to save your current progress to local storage. Use the "Load Game" button to restore previously saved progress.
What devices does the game support?
Our game supports all modern browsers and devices, including desktop computers, tablets, and smartphones.
How do I report a game bug?
Please use the contact form above and select "Bug Report" as the subject, then describe the issue in detail including your device and browser information.
Can we collaborate on new features?
We welcome all partnership opportunities! Please use the contact form above and select "Partnership Opportunity" as the subject, then describe your ideas in detail.
